About RETHERAPH

Retheraph comes from the word "therapist".
Our goal is to allow our app users to check the mental status frequently.
The users can check their mental status by answering 6 questions, whose questions are all related to mental disorders, such as Anxiety disorders, Major depressive disorder, and Bipolar disorder.
We are hoping that Retheraph can be the users' therapist by showing the potential illnesses that our users may suffer.
